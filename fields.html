Land & Weapon Systems

Land & Weapon Systems

Our mission is to identify the needs for advanced land-based weapon, electronic, and electro-optical systems that enhance precision, survivability, and situational awareness on the battlefield, and to participate in their design and integration to ensure their provision to relevant users at the highest level. We specialize in electro-optical countermeasures systems, infrared optics, laser-based technologies (including designators, pointers, rangefinders, and directed energy applications), and weapon aiming solutions for main battle tanks, infantry fighting vehicles, missile launchers, and remotely controlled weapon systems. Through our innovative reconnaissance, surveillance, and handheld electro-optical solutions, we deliver reliable, high-performance capabilities that support modern land forces in achieving a decisive operational advantage.

Air Defense Technologies

Air Defense Technologies

A comprehensive Air and Missile Defense (AMD) mission involves integrating layered defense capabilities to protect critical assets and personnel from all incoming aerial threats, including aircraft, unmanned aerial vehicles, cruise missiles, and ballistic missiles. This requires a sophisticated system utilizing long-range radar detection, command and control coordination, and various interception technologies. Within this layered architecture, specialized air and missile artillery systems, employing high-caliber cannons and short-range rockets, provide the necessary, highly responsive point defense to neutralize threats that penetrate outer defense perimeters. The overall objective is to achieve and maintain continuous battlefield superiority and ensure the integrity of protected airspace through the coordinated detection, tracking, and destruction of these threats at optimal altitudes and ranges.

Advanced avionic systems and sophisticated navigation systems in modern aircraft ensure safe, efficient, and reliable flight operations across all global airspace.

These systems use a comprehensive suite of technologies—from satellite-based GPS and ground-based radio navigation aids (like VOR and ILS) to self-contained Inertial Navigation Systems (INS)—to provide pilots and automated flight management systems with precise positioning, situational awareness, and robust guidance. They work together to reduce pilot workload, enhance safety, and enable complex maneuvers and landings in diverse environmental conditions.

Naval Defense Technologies

Naval Defense Technologies

Naval Defense Technologies is committed to providing modern, high-tech, and integrated solutions for naval platforms. In this context, it aims to enhance maritime security and operational effectiveness by developing electronic warfare, radar, sonar, weapon systems, command and control, and navigation solutions for surface and underwater systems. With these systems developed using reliable technologies, the company aims to provide naval forces with strategic capabilities to meet future needs and to maintain a competitive position internationally.

Space Technologies

Space Technologies

Space Technologies has made it its mission to develop critical subsystems for satellite systems, ground stations, space platforms, and space missions, with the aim of increasing countries' capabilities in space. In this context, it aims to produce competitive solutions nationally and internationally and to make strategic contributions to the space ecosystem by providing high-performance, reliable, and innovative space technologies for communication, observation, and scientific research.

Cyber Technologies

Cyber Technologies

The Cyber Technologies group is committed to identifying and supplying the necessary cybersecurity products, solutions, and services to protect national and critical infrastructures against cyber threats. In this context, it aims to ensure the cyber independence and security of nations by offering innovative and domestically developed technologies in areas such as cyber intelligence, cyber defense, cyber attack, and cybersecurity operations centers, while also creating a competitive cybersecurity ecosystem on an international level.
