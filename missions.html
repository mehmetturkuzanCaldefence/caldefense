Our Missions

Mission-driven delivery across land, air defense, naval, space, and cyber domains.

Mission

Land & Weapon Systems Mission

To identify the needs for advanced land-based weapon, electronic, and electro-optical systems that enhance precision, survivability, and situational awareness on the battlefield, and to participate in their design and integration to ensure their provision to relevant users at the highest level.

Mission

Air Defense Technologies Mission

To provide comprehensive Air and Missile Defense capabilities that protect critical assets and personnel from all incoming aerial threats through sophisticated detection, tracking, command and control coordination, and interception technologies — achieving continuous battlefield superiority.

Mission

Naval Defense Technologies Mission

To deliver modern, high-tech, and integrated solutions for naval platforms that enhance maritime security and operational effectiveness through electronic warfare, radar, sonar, weapon systems, command and control, and navigation solutions.

Mission

Space Technologies Mission

To develop critical subsystems for satellite systems, ground stations, space platforms, and space missions, increasing national capabilities in space and producing competitive solutions for communication, observation, and scientific research.

Mission

Cyber Technologies Mission

To identify and supply necessary cybersecurity products, solutions, and services that protect national and critical infrastructures against cyber threats, ensuring cyber independence and security through innovative and domestically developed technologies.
